Cover Letter

Why I want to work at Baldwin&

Joshua Pekera

STRATEGY. MARKETING. BRANDING. PRODUCT DESIGNER. IXD. UXD. UID.

  • JOSHUAPEKERA@ME.COM
  • 205.534.6769

Bonjour!

To Baldwin&,

Melissa Wheeler's blog post inspired me to create this website to express my interest in the UX Designer and Senior Art Director positions at Luckie & Company.

Ideation takes fearless passion. However, it is the diversity of our combined life experiences that champion an idea and see it realized in exceptional creative work that tells a story. "The broader one's understanding of the human experience, the better design we will have." Words wisely shared by Steve Jobs when explaining how a designer conceives an idea.

The collective talents and experiences at your agency fuel the ideation of stories that build brands. Your desire to tell stories across every medium fosters a company culture that embraces emerging media. I find your outlook towards the changing media landscape coupled with such an innovative team to be exciting and rare. I would welcome the opportunity to learn from such a talented team and contribute to your insightful work.

As I am approaching my 29th birthday, I feel that my experiences during the last six years of working professionally have been atypical for someone my age. As a college graduate, a mentor recruited me to go to KSI, Inc. A manufacturer of custom environmental graphics and ornamental structures, KSI's installations are placed in themed sites such as Universal Studios and Dallas Cowboys Stadium. The two years that followed consisted of 12 hour work days, many business trips, a baptism by fire into the construction industry and the opportunity to work with some brilliant people.

The changing landscape of advertising coupled with the profound growth of social media, mobile devices and rich web applications gave me aspirations to pursue my true passion. In 2009, I saw an opportunity to start my own boutique digital agency. This ultimately brought me to my current position as Creative Director at SM Agency. In that time, I also cofounded The Agorean, an online social news organization with its own apparel line.

The last three years as Creative Director at SM Agency have allowed me to master several creative disciplines in the traditional and digital landscape. Since buying into partnership in 2009, the company has seen tremendous financial growth from its creative services and digital products. My position allowed me to recruit and work with some of the best creative talent I have ever encountered. My time at SM has been an invaluable experience that has prepared me to take the next step in my career.

My dream is to work with national brands and develop disruptive campaigns that utilize emerging media. I have a fervency for crafting beautifully branded products that merge art and technology. I approach every product with the enthusiasm to make it engage, inspire and leave a lasting impression on a user with the story it tells. I would like to bring my passion to Luckie and contribute to the stories you tell that build brands.

Your time is valuable so thank you for reading through this entire letter. I hope you will consider me a candidate for either the UX Designer or Senior Art Director positions. I have included a link to my portfolio for you to review. I would be happy to answer any questions you may have for me. I hope to hear from you soon.

Sincerely,

Joshua Pekera
