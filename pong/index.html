Lets Play Some Pong!

This is just like the Pong you play with your friends on vintage video game night on your Atari. Hmmm...that might just be me, but either way it is simple and fun! Play against a friend or the computer. The first person to score 9 points is the winner. Read the instructions below on how to start playing and how to control your paddle.

How to Play

How to Start

  • Press 1 for a single player game.
  • Press 2 for a multi player game.
  • Press 0 to watch the computer play itself.

Game Controls

  • Player 1 moves using the Q and A keys.
  • Player 2 moves using the P and L keys.
  • Esc can be used to abandon a game.
